Popular Help Topics

  • Reset online banking password ▼
    Click “Forgot Password” on the login page and follow the prompts
  • Find your account number ▼
    Your account number is listed on your bank statements and inside the mobile app under “Account Details”
  • Report a lost or stolen card ▼
    Call our 24/7 helpline immediately or block your card through the Acme Bank mobile app
  • Customer service hours ▼
    Phone support is available 24/7, and chat support is available from 8 AM–10 PM daily
  • Activate a new debit/credit card ▼
    Activate it instantly using the Acme Bank mobile app or by calling the number on the back of your card

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I open a new account with Acme Bank? ▼
    You can apply online through our website or visit any Acme Bank branch. You’ll need a valid ID, proof of address, and your basic personal information. Most online applications are approved within minutes.
  • How long do money transfers take? ▼
    Domestic transfers between Acme Bank accounts are usually instant. Transfers to other banks may take 1–3 business days depending on the receiving bank’s processing time.
  • Are there fees for international transactions? ▼
    Yes, international transactions may incur foreign exchange fees and service charges. You can view the exact rates in your account’s “Fees & Charges” section before making a transaction.
  • What should I do if I suspect fraud on my account? ▼
    Immediately lock your card using the Acme Bank app or call our fraud hotline. Our security team will investigate, and you won’t be held responsible for unauthorized transactions reported promptly.
  • How can I increase my credit card limit? ▼
    You can request a limit increase through the mobile app or by calling customer service. Approval depends on your credit history, repayment track record, and overall account standing.
